Prompt Design Rationale:

Narrative AI: The focus on this prompt design was to guide the game in a way that was both fun and immersive for the player. Focus had to be placed on the creativity
needed for the story immersion. A focus also had to be placed on danger on conflict, as those were things that would drive decisions for the player. Any other prompting
decisions was so that the AI didn't stray off track.

Inventory System and Health System: These 2 will be put together as they are very similar. The focus on these prompts was much different because they had to be accurate and
not creative. Any prompting decisions made here was to ensure accuracy within a broad set of use cases. and then to handle edge cases that may give the model problems.

Key Design Decisions:
  - For ease of use, health potions fill hp to max
     This allows for little regulation on how often hp potions are acquired, while still making them feel incredibly valuable
  - Enemies do a very varied, yet possibly large amount of damage
      As a player, you must mitigate risk, as you can be killed in 2 hits periodically. This makes the world feel stronger, and scarier
  - Death is permanent
      This pushes players to try many different adventures and experiences, instead of focusing on 1. If a leveling system/progression was added, this would be changed.
